How to Play

  • 2 Players take turns on the same screen. Red goes first.
  • Pieces move diagonally forward one square onto dark tiles.
  • Capture by jumping over an opponent's piece diagonally. The jumped piece is removed.
  • Captures are mandatory. If you can capture, you must. Pieces that must capture will pulse.
  • Multi-jump: if after a capture another capture is available with the same piece, you must continue.
  • When a piece reaches the far row, it becomes a King (crowned with K). Kings can move and capture diagonally in all 4 directions.
  • You win by capturing all opponent pieces or leaving them with no legal moves.
