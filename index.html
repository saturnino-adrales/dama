How to Play

  • Pieces move diagonally forward one square onto dark tiles.
  • Capture by jumping over an opponent's piece diagonally (forward or backward).
  • Captures are mandatory. Pieces that must capture will pulse.
  • Multi-jump: if another capture is available after jumping, you must continue.
  • King (K): reaches the far row. Moves and captures any distance diagonally (flying king).
  • Win by capturing all enemy pieces or blocking all their moves.
